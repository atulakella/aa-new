About Me

Hey there! I’m Atul Akella, an Electronics and Communications Engineering student who loves building things. I’m not just limited to ECE—I consider myself a "maker" at heart. Ever since I was young, I’ve been drawn to technology, and over the years, I’ve worked on projects using hardware like Arduino, ESP32, Raspberry Pi, and NVIDIA Jetson, along with programming skills in C++ and Python.

I’m really interested in robotics, AI, and machine learning, and I’m always learning new things in these areas. I’ve also explored ROS2, which has helped me take on some exciting robotics projects. Apart from my core field, I’m curious about machine learning, backend development, APIs, CAD design, 3D printing, and networking. I’m even into self-hosting my own projects—it’s a fun challenge!

When I’m not working on projects, I love participating in hackathons and CTFs, and as a freshman. If you’re into tech or just want to chat about cool projects, feel free to reach out. Let’s connect!

A few things I'm proud of...

These are a few achievements that make me smile when I look back. Each one reflects my curiosity and passion for building and creating. From making my first robot at 13 to representing my school at IIT Kharagpur, these moments remind me of the challenges I faced and the lessons I learned. They inspire me to keep exploring and creating new things every day.

Built my first robot at age 13

I started my journey into the world of robotics with sheer curiosity, I made my first Bluetooth-controlled robotic arm boasting three degrees of freedom, which ignited my passion for technology and innovation. I uploaded a video demonstration of it on YouTube.

Developed first portfolio website at age 15

I honed my skills further by designing and developing my personal portfolio website. Fueled by the desire to showcase my projects and accomplishments, also network with people. Furthermore I made a playful chatbot equipped with knowledge about me and a little sense of humor, adds an interactive element to my digital presence.

Represented school at IIT Kharagpur at 14

I represented my school at the prestigious IIT-Kharagpur. Leading my team to the finals of the pan Asia Young Innovator’s Program (YIP 2019), hosted by IIT Kharagpur, was an honor. Amongst thousands of teams, we stood out, securing a position in the top 30 with our innovative approach to address vehicular pollution.

Started professional journey at 18

I began my professional journey as a Machine Learning Solutions Engineer at ShrimpIQ, a startup focusing on real-time innovations to revolutinize the aquaculture industry. Applying my machine learning skills to real-world challenges was both exciting and fulfilling.

Won three hackathons as a college freshman

As a college freshman, I proudly won three hackathons, one of which was held at IIT Roorkee, showcasing my ability to think creatively and tackle real-world challenges in a competitive environment.

The Ghee Project

Moved by the Tirupati laddu controversy, where beef tallow was reportedly mixed into the sacred prasadam, I started developing an electronic nose to differentiate between pure desi ghee and adulterated ghee. So far, I've achieved 68% accuracy and am actively working on improving its precision.
